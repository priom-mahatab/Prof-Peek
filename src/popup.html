Prof Peek SEU

Prof Peek seamlessly integrates RateMyProfessors ratings directly into the St. Edward's course catalog table, allowing you to quickly evaluate instructors while planning your schedule.

A combined rating of 0 and difficulty 0 means that the extension was unable to find any data on the Professor.

Disclaimer: Ratings and difficulty scores are provided from RateMyProfessors and may not reflect the full classroom experience. Please use this extension as a supplement, not a substitute, for your own judgment and research when selecting courses or instructors.
